The culinary secrets of the medieval citadel and the lovely Saxon villages around it

SIGHIȘOARA FOOD TOUR

  • SIGHIȘOARA WALKING FOOD TOUR
  • ½ DAY, 4,5 HOURS
  • MIN. 4 – MAX. 12 PERS.
  • MON - SUN, APR. - OCT.
  • EUR 35 – ADULTS
    EUR 17,5 – CHILDREN & ADOLESCENTS
    (7 to 17 years)
    Free – Children (4 to 6 years)

Târnave wines, water buffalos and old cake recipes

Our walking food tour takes you during the 4.5 hours from through the UNESCO protected citadel (Burg) and to the Lower old centre. You will have the chance to taste some exclusive local products and try our recipes.


Our tour highlights include:

Learn more about how nature and the territory influences the local food, we will talk about the characteristics of our gastronomy in the oldest school of Sighișoara.

Try some typical cheesefrom the Târnave area of the Transylvanian Highlands and our delicious pork specialities.

Taste some local white wines, such as the Fetească Regală (Royal Maiden), from a small, local wine producer.

Get an introduction in our various sweet and sour soups, the everyday meal of the majority of the Transylvanians.

Experience an old Saxon recipe, reinterpreted by an open-minded chef from a local restaurant.

Sample the local fruit alcoholic drinks, a must during a visit in Transylvania.

Visit some of the unseen corners of historical Sighișoara.

Try some of the most representative sweet recipes, cakes, baked by the guide himself.


The price includes the food and drink tastings as well as the English-speaking guide. You’ll also receive a map of Sighișoara and five old recipes from the area. Transportation to and from the starting point of food tour is not included. We also recommend you to bring a bottle of water as we walk for a while. Our tour will be outside for most of the time.

Bike tour with locals to a culinary event in the rural area of Târnave

This is a tour for active people as we must pedal some 3-4 hours to our destination and after the brunch back again ;).

The route takes us through the nice scenery of the Transylvanian Highlands and many small villages, some of them almost forgotten. This local group gathers every second weekend during the warm season and visits local farms, family husbandries or just some small initiatives where agriculture and food production is important.


The price includes:

  • bike rental – Merida MTB (please send us information about you size and weight)
  • English speaking guide
  • Food and drinks at the destination
  • Bike Map of the Transylvanian Highlands
